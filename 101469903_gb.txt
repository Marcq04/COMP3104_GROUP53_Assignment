George Brown College
Location: Toronto, Ontario, Canada
Founded: 1967

Overview:
George Brown College is a leading public post-secondary institution offering a wide range of programs in various fields, including business, health sciences, technology, and the arts.

Campuses:

Casa Loma
St. James

Programs Offered:

Over 160 programs, including diplomas, certificates, degrees, and postgraduate programs.
Industry-focused programs providing hands-on experience.
Preparation for careers in high-demand industries.
Reputation:
The college is known for its practical, hands-on learning approach and strong industry partnerships, which help students gain real-world experience. Over the years, George Brown College has expanded its offerings and facilities to meet the evolving needs of students and the workforce, establishing itself as one of Toronto's leading institutions.

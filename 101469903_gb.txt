George Brown College is a public institution located in Toronto, Ontario. 
It offers a wide range of programs across several fields including business, 
health sciences, technology, and arts. The college was founded in 1967 and 
has grown to include three main campuses: Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront.

George Brown is known for its industry-focused programs that provide students with hands-on experience.
